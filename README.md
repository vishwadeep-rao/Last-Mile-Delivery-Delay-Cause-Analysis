📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analytics solution to identify the key factors contributing to delays in last-mile delivery operations. Using a real-world logistics dataset containing 2,080 delivery records across 10 Indian cities, the analysis uncovers operational bottlenecks through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical hypothesis testing, and interactive visualizations.

The objective is to transform raw delivery data into actionable business insights that can help logistics companies improve delivery performance, optimize rider allocation, and enhance customer satisfaction.

🎯 Business Problem

Last-mile delivery is one of the most critical and expensive stages of the logistics process. Delivery delays negatively impact customer experience, increase operational costs, and reduce overall efficiency.

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

Are deliveries during peak traffic hours significantly more delayed than off-peak deliveries?
How do weather conditions influence delivery delays?
Does rider experience significantly affect delivery performance?
Which cities and vehicle types require operational improvements?
